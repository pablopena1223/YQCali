Hello! My name is Yanelly Quintanilla, I’m a New York City-based calligrapher, lettering artist, and recent graphic designer.

Growing up, my parents always pushed me to have nice penmanship and eventually from there I learned how to write in script. As I got older, I became interested in lettering and design, but it wasn’t until my senior year of high school where I fully immersed myself in art. I have been specializing in hand-lettering and chalk art for well over 5 years, with developing skills in graphic design. A great deal is put into the art of drawing letters, in order to create eye-catching signs.

Thankfully, after starting my small calligraphy business, I've been able to work closely with clients such as Starbucks, Grace's Marketplace, and have helped many clients have a little piece of art for their special occasions.

Lettering and design is truly my passion; if there’s a canvas to work on, I’m always up for the task.
